Infographic

ArcPro | Microsoft Excel | Adobe Illustrator

I created this infographic from idea conception to finished product. First, I located and analyzed data from the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics, American Community Survey, and Annual Survey of School System Finances in excel. Next, I joined tables to shapefiles in ArcPro to create a bivariate choropleth map and table visualization. Finally, I took my analysis and research into Adobe Illustrator to create an infographic as a learning resource.
