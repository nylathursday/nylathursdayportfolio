Pacific Northwest Terrain

ArcPro | Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Illustrator

Using a classified landcover shapefile and a DEM in ArcPro I use the spatial analyst toolbox to create hillshade layers with varying intensity. I then blended the layers in photoshop to create a terrain basemap and moved the basemap into illustrator to add ocean bathymetry, rivers, and lakes.
